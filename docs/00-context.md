# Project Context - Our Journey ❤️

You are helping build a Flutter mobile application called "Our Journey".

## Product Vision

Our Journey is a digital scrapbook and relationship album designed specifically for couples in Bolivia.

Unlike generic couple apps that focus on counting days together, Our Journey focuses on collecting real experiences and memories.

Users complete activities, challenges, and date experiences across different Bolivian departments and document those moments with photos, notes, ratings, and completion dates.

The app should feel like a mix between:

* A travel journal
* A scrapbook
* A relationship album
* A collectible experience tracker

The emotional goal is to encourage couples to create memories together while exploring Bolivia.

---

## Target Audience

* Couples aged 18-35
* Bolivian users
* Android and iOS users
* Users who enjoy travel, memories, and relationship experiences

---

## Core Concept

The application is organized by Bolivian departments.

Each department contains a curated list of experiences.

Examples:

La Paz:

* Ride the Teleférico together
* Visit Valle de la Luna
* Watch the Illimani at sunrise

Cochabamba:

* Eat a silpancho together
* Visit Cristo de la Concordia

Santa Cruz:

* Watch the sunset at Río Piraí
* Try traditional cuñapé

Users complete experiences and gradually build a memory collection.

---

## MVP Scope

Users can:

* Browse departments
* Browse experiences
* Mark experiences as completed
* Add photos
* Add notes
* Add completion dates
* Rate experiences
* Track progress

The MVP does NOT require:

* Authentication
* Cloud sync
* Social features
* AI features
* Online backend

The MVP must work completely offline.

## Design Philosophy

The application should feel:

* Warm
* Romantic
* Modern
* Elegant
* Emotional
* Inspired by scrapbooks and travel journals

Avoid childish designs.

Prioritize usability and emotional storytelling.

## Development Rules
